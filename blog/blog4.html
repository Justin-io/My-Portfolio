Building Offline-First PWAs

Nov 30, 2024 • Hari Nandan K

We often assume users have 5G. They don't. When building F.R.I.D.A.Y., my goal was zero-dependency: a single HTML file that works in the middle of a desert.

Service Workers 101

The Service Worker is a network proxy that lives in the browser. By intercepting fetch requests, we can serve cached assets instantly. For F.R.I.D.A.Y., I used a "Cache-First" strategy for assets and "Network-First" for data updates, ensuring the app loads in under 500ms.

Local Persistence

IndexedDB and localStorage allow us to save complex state on the device. I implemented a robust sync engine that queues changes when offline and replays them when the connection is restored.
